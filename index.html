We Were soldiers once, and fit

Six years later (20NOV2019 23:25)

In 2013 I was at the pinnacle of my physical humanity. I could do 75 pushups and 82 situps under two minutes respectively and then hit two miles under 16 minutes without breaking a sweat. Fast forward six years, a wife, two kids, a radical career change, and a new hometown and I wake up in cold sweats in the middle of the night because I know I can't pass my APFT and I'm afraid of what Drill Sergeant Barton is going to do to my soul when I get to hell. Why would I be in hell? Well because of the immortal sin of failing the APFT and not being fit for duty.
